  • Progressive Enhancement Writing Assignment

    Progressive enhancement is a web developement approach that starts with a solid foundation. Mainly focusing on clean, accessible content and then adding layers of visually appealing designs and advanced functionlity for that browser that can support it. Unlike graceful degradation, which prioritizes modern browsers and only patches support for older ones, progressive enhancement ensures that all users—regardless of device or browser—can access the core content and functionality. This method promotes inclusivity, accessibility, and long-term resilience, making it a thoughtful and future-friendly way to build websites.
